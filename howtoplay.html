Gamefolio

Personal channels are pretty cool, and most people have one, but I have noticed that a lot of personal websites look very similar, and are very bland. I challenge you to remake your portfolio site in the style of your favorite game!

Requirements:

- Website must inform the reader about you, and show off your skills/projects.

- Website must have your socials and/or ways to contact you.

- Website must be interactive, in the style of a game.

- You must log at least four hours using wakatime.
